Attire - Dressy Casual

The ceremony and reception will take place at the Aldo Leopold Nature Center. The venue is a nature-focused center with hiking trails and indoor exhibits. Weather permitting, the ceremony will take place outside – please dress accordingly to walk around and enjoy the outdoors. Following the ceremony, guests can take time to explore the grounds, play lawn games, and listen to the band during cocktail hour. Dinner, dancing, and a bonfire will follow!



Sunday - Everyone is welcome to join us at the Memorial Union Terrace between 10:00 and noon on Sunday for a final get-together. There are breakfast and lunch options available inside the Union, as well as coffee, ice cream, beer & wine. If the weather is nice, we will be out on the terrace, otherwise inside Der Rathskeller
