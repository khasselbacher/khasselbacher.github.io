Wedding

The ceremony and reception will take place at the Aldo Leopold Nature. The venue is a nature-focused center with hiking trails as well as indoor exhibits. Weather permitting, the ceremony will take place outside – please dress accordingly to walk around and enjoy the outdoors. Following the ceremony, guests can take time to explore the grounds, play lawn games, and listen to a band during cocktail hour. Dinner, dancing, and a bonfire will follow!
